About the Practice

Castleton Dental Practice has been part of the Farnham community since 1973. Under new ownership, we are building on that heritage — same trusted team, new energy and investment.

Our Story

Castleton is a fully private and independently owned dental practice. Our long-established team brings continuity and consistency to every appointment — because familiarity and trust are the foundations of good dental care.

We are an independent practice, which means all decisions are made within the same small team, under one roof. Our manager and clinical leads are always present and accessible. There are no chains of command, no corporate processes — just a dedicated team focused on patient care.

Our technology reflects that commitment. Our iTero digital scanner almost eliminates the need for traditional impressions, reducing patient discomfort and producing more accurate results. We were among the first practices in the region to offer GBT Guided Biofilm Therapy — the gold standard in dental hygiene.

Our Ethos

At Castleton, we want every patient to feel comfortable and at ease in professional, capable hands. Our priority is patient care — from our highly qualified clinical team to our state-of-the-art facilities. Every member of our team, including reception, is fully qualified and registered with the General Dental Council.

We believe in honest, transparent treatment planning. If there are any questions or concerns at any stage of your experience, your treatment coordinator is readily available by phone and email.
