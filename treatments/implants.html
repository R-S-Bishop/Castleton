Specialist

Implants & Oral Surgery

Dental implants replace missing teeth with titanium posts that integrate with the jawbone, providing a permanent, stable foundation for crowns that look and function like natural teeth.

What to Expect

Implants are placed by our implantologist Dr. Caoimhe Doherty. The process begins with an initial consultation, followed by detailed treatment planning. The titanium post is placed surgically; over time, the bone grows around and bonds with the implant to hold it securely. The final crown is fitted once integration is complete.

We also offer CBCT scanning and accept referrals for implant placement, oral surgery, and IV sedation cases from practices across the region. If you are a referring dentist, please visit our Referrals page for details.
