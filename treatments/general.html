Routine Care

General & Hygiene

Preventative dentistry is the foundation of a healthy mouth. Regular examinations, professional hygiene, and personalised advice help you avoid problems before they develop — and keep your smile looking and feeling its best.

What to Expect

We offer full comprehensive examinations including radiographs and detailed oral hygiene advice for the whole family. During these appointments we will also advise on the most up-to-date dietary guidance and efficient oral hygiene techniques to help prevent tooth decay and gum disease at home.

We are a GBT (Guided Biofilm Therapy) certified practice. Using EMS Airflow technology, our hygienists use a combination of air, powder, and water to gently remove biofilm with exceptional precision — more comfortable and more thorough than traditional scaling alone.

Fissure sealants are also available, particularly for children as their adult teeth come through. A thin protective coating applied to the biting surfaces of the back teeth, sealants protect against decay in the naturally occurring grooves where bacteria can accumulate.
